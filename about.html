About Me

I'm an individual with a strong foundation in computer science and a keen interest in Web Development and Information Technology. My journey began when I decided to try out Cybersecurity and Web Development at the Center for Advanced Research and Technology (CART) back in high-school. Over the years, I have spent majority of my time dedicated towards my education, but am now diving back into the field of combining Web-Dev, IT, and even UX immersions together.

Full-Stack Development Pathway

On my journey to becoming a proficient Full-Stack Developer, I have actively been working on my free time, learning experiences and the skills necessary to excel in both front-end and back-end development. This includes mastering technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript for front-end development, Node.js, Express.js, and databases like MongoDB for back-end development. I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends to ensure that I can deliver high-quality, efficient, and user-friendly web applications.
